Term Definition
center pile In the game of BS, this is where players submit their cards to try to empty their hands and win the game.
contract This is a description of a function. A contract states the function's preconditions, its postconditions, whether or not it has a unit test, its parameters (and descriptions of them), what it returns, and what it throws (e.g. exception).
hover This refers to having one's cursor over a card, allowing him/her to pick the card if he/she wants to.
pick This refers to selecting a card to submit to the center pile.
